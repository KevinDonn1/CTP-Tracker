CTP Flag Tracker

Free tool for disc golf leagues — assigns which groups bring out and pick up CTP flags based on starting holes.

Instructions & How to Use (First-Time Users)

This app helps disc golf league directors quickly assign CTP flag duties (who brings out the flag as the first group to a hole, and who picks it up as the last). It's customizable for any course and number of holes.

  1. Enter your course name (optional, for display).
  2. Select or enter the total number of holes (e.g., 18 for standard courses).
  3. Enter group starting holes as comma-separated numbers (e.g., 1,5,10 for 3 groups).
  4. Enter CTP holes as comma-separated numbers (e.g., 2,4,6,8 — any number of CTPS).
  5. Click "Generate Flag Assignments" — output shows per-group duties.
  6. Share the link or screenshot the results. Works on phones too!

Tip: The app calculates based on a circular layout (groups wrap around). For questions, contact the creator on Reddit/Facebook.
